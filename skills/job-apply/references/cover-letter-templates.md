# Cover Letter Templates

3-5 sentences. Two short paragraphs. 100-160 words. Customize the bracketed parts for each role. Never send verbatim.

**Banned words** (never use): excited, passionate, thrilled, leverage, synergy, innovative, cutting-edge, world-class, dynamic, rockstar, guru, thought leader, disruptive, Lmk, feel free, reach out, circle back, hit the ground running, move the needle, great fit, perfect fit, exclamation points.

## Template 1: AI/ML Roles

```
Hi [name],

At Levee I built a computer vision system to 92%+ accuracy across 10,000+ hotel rooms, cutting inspection time 60% during a Marriott pilot. [Company]'s work on [specific AI initiative] is the same class of problem — production AI with measurable operational impact.

I've since extended that into full-stack AI platforms: multi-tenant SaaS, real-time dashboards, and LLM integrations in Next.js/Supabase. Both the ML and the product layer are areas I can contribute to from day one.

Guillermo Villegas
```
